# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- being respectful in language and tone
- accepting and giving constructive feedback
- focusing discussions on technical outcomes
- assuming good intent while clarifying disagreements

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing or publishing private information
- trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to maintainers through GitHub maintainers contact channels.

Maintainers may remove or edit content and, where needed, restrict participation.
